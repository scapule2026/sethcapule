Wikipedia defines a meme as an idea, behavior, or style that spreads from person to person, and carries a meaning that represents a certain phenomenon or theme.

A Way to Express

Memes are a way to express common interests with other people offline and online. They provide great insight to the world around us and encompasses a sense of unity altogether.

A Way to Communicate

Memes are a way to talk and relate to other people through shared media. It can help us find what similarities we have with each other through the use of humor and relatability.

A Way to Laugh

Memes are also a primary source of entertainment found throughout many social platforms such as Youtube or TikTok. Because of the wide variety of memes, it won't take long until you find a meme that makes you laugh.
