Kratos X Jesus (2022)


Features: Kratos, Religion
Description: Kratos, a character from an iconic game of the 2000's and of the recent hit, God of War Ragnarok, is featured in a hypothetical interaction with Jesus Christ himself. This meme aims to ask the question of what would occur when a man of a broken past meets the son of God, and also how Christianity could influence the God of War storyline.






Lightskin Stare (2013-Current)


Features: Metro Man from Megamind
Description: Originated from Vine in 2013, it was first popularized by Viners Melvin Gregg and King Bach. Eventually, it spread to TikTok and also used several figures in the Megamind series such as Metro Man and Hal Stewart as a display of the stare.


Kanye Made Graduation (2022-Current)


Features: Kanye West, Graduation
Description: "Kanye made Graduation" is a cliche catchphrase used by devotees of infamous rapper, Kanye West, to defend his several contoversies such as his antisemitic remarks in recent posts. This meme also tends to poke fun at the delusional state of his fans who acknowledge his actions.


Ohio (2016-Current)


Features: Ohio
Description: Ohio is a state in America with a wide variety of geographical features, and a wide array of memes surrounding it. The first traces of Ohio related memes were from 2016 with the introduction of the Ohio vs. the World trend, and later on resurfacing around 2020. Recently, the Ohio memes have seemed to label Ohio as a desolate wasteland of creepypasta and mysterious lore, perhaps as a way to highlight the chaotic nature of modern memes.


Pootis Status (2022-Current)


Features: Team Fortress, Metal Gear Rising
Description: On February 19th, 2022, a user by the name of ceno0 uploaded a video titled "Pootis Engage // EXTREME" which showcases fights between several icons of the Steam game, Team Fortress 2. A clip from the video was later used for the first official "pootis status" video, and quickly spread to Youtube. The meme is a conglomeration of the iconic "POOTIS" meme from Team Fortress, as well as the "Revengeance Status" meme from 2021.
